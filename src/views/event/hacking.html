Submit your hack

We use Devpost for hack submissions.

To Devpost

How to submit your hack

  • Every member of your team needs to have a Devpost account.
  • Only one person needs to submit the hack: to do this click 'Enter a Submission'.
    1. Provide a good overview of what you've developed in the description field. This will be used as criteria to assess your hack. Do use screenshots if you wish.
    2. Enter your team members' Devpost accounts.
    3. Choose the challenge or challenges to enter for.

    Rules

  • You must submit your hack before hacking ends at 12:45:00 GMT
  • You must submit all relevant source code
