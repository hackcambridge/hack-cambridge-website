Recruiting

After reviewing each applicant’s CV at least twice, we only admit the best candidates to Hack Cambridge. We set a high standard to ensure our attendees have impressive technical prowess, a passionate entrepreneurial spirit, and a proven record of innovation. This is a truly unique opportunity for you: 24 hours straight to work with the next generation of talent, evaluating their technical abilities and cultural fit with your organisation. Bring your best recruiters and an unlimited number of mentors – with such a strong pool of candidates, you’ll be sure to find the perfect hire!
